About

With over 15 years of experience, I have thrived in the dynamic construction sector. As a Senior Planner at McLaren Construction Group in London, I have had the privilege of making significant contributions to a diverse range of projects. My expertise spans a broad spectrum of construction endeavours, including local authority residential schemes, high-rise boutique hotels, data centres, shopping centres, large-scale exhibition facilities, and city-centre office buildings.

My educational background complements my extensive experience. A Master’s Degree in Innovation Management (MBA) sharpens my strategic thinking, while my Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology solidifies my technical skills. Additionally, an NVQ Level 6 in Construction Management ensures a deep understanding of industry practices. This unique combination enables me to approach project planning with fresh perspectives and innovative solutions.

In addition to my technical proficiency, I am devoted to achieving professional excellence. As a member of the Chartered Institute of Building (CIOB), I am committed to staying informed about industry best practices and contributing to the advancement of our field.
